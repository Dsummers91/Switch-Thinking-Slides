Privacy

Every transaction on blockchain is public

Everyone can see your entire account history and balance

Speed

Most blockchains are very slow

  • Bitcoin - 10 minute block time
  • Ethereum - 14 second block time

Randomness

Blockchains are inherently deterministic.

Most contracts may used pseudo-random variables such as blockhash

          
          bytes32 blockHash = blockhash(block.number - 1);
          uint32 winner = uint32(blockHash) % numOfTickets;

But miners always have final say

Race Conditions

There is an inescapable time delay between the moment a blockchain transaction is broadcast to the network and the moment that that same transaction is mined into a block. During the intervening period, transactions sit in a pending transaction pool (mempool) waiting to be mined.
